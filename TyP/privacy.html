Privacy Policy – Rutana

Last updated: October 2025
Your Privacy Matters to Us.

At QoriTech, we respect your privacy and protect your personal data. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard the information of Rutana users.

Information Collected

We collect data necessary for the system’s operation, including: Account information (name, email, encrypted password). Fleet and route data (vehicles, locations, delivery statuses). Technical information (IP address, device, and browser).

Use of Information

The information is used for: Operating, maintaining, and improving the service. Personalizing the user experience. Analyzing metrics and generating performance reports. Administrative communications and technical support.

Protection of Information

We apply technical and organizational security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse of data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties, except when necessary for the service’s operation (e.g., cloud providers or notifications).

User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us at privacy@qoritech.com.

Data Retention

Data will be retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes described or while the account remains active.

Changes to the Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Modifications will be published with the corresponding revision date.
